House Family System

Your House is your home away from home, providing a close-knit community where friendships flourish and support is always available.

  • Dedicated Housemaster/Housemistress providing 24/7 support
  • Experienced Matrons and resident Tutors
  • House-based activities building strong relationships
  • Cross-year group mentoring and friendship
  • Regular House meetings and one-to-one check-ins

Personal Tutor System

Every pupil has a dedicated Tutor who knows them personally and supports their academic and personal development throughout Lower College.

  • Personal Tutor remains with your child until Fifth Form
  • Daily morning meetings and fortnightly one-to-ones
  • First port of call for all academic matters
  • Delivery of Floreat wellbeing programme
  • Regular communication with parents and House staff

Floreat Wellbeing Programme

Our comprehensive PSHE programme focuses on personal development, mental health awareness, relationships, and preparing for life beyond College.

  • Holistic development: Myself, Myself & Others, Myself & the World
  • Mental health and stress management techniques
  • Digital health and online safety
  • Relationships and sex education
  • Exam preparation and revision skills
  • Equality, inclusion and community responsibility

Health Centre Support

Professional healthcare and counselling services ensure both physical and mental wellbeing are prioritised at all times.

  • Qualified medical staff and counsellors on-site
  • Drop-in support available for immediate needs
  • Professional counselling and mental health support
  • Health education and wellness programmes
  • Confidential support for sensitive issues

Peer Mentoring & EDI

Our Bold & Kind peer mentoring programme and commitment to Equality, Diversity & Inclusion ensures every pupil feels valued and supported.

  • Peer mentoring across year groups
  • LGBTQ+ support through Pride and Allies group
  • Anti-racism education and support networks
  • Cultural celebration and awareness programmes
  • Bystander intervention training

Learning Support

Comprehensive academic support ensures every pupil can reach their full potential, regardless of their individual learning needs.

  • Initial literacy and learning assessments
  • Individual learning support plans
  • Specialist one-to-one tuition available
  • Exam access arrangements coordination
  • Study skills and revision programmes
  • English as Additional Language support
